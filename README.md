# Light Diary
A integrated personal digital diary

Do you want to keep a diary of your life, thoughts, and feelings? Do you want to have a convenient and secure way to record and access your diary entries anytime, anywhere? If yes, then you might be interested in Light Diary, a simple yet powerful diary application that lets you write, edit, and organize your diary in rich text format.
With Light Diary, you can:

* Write your diary entries in **rich text** format, using different fonts, colors, sizes, styles, and alignments. You can also insert  lists, links, and other elements to make your diary more expressive and attractive.

* Organize your diary entries in a **daily and document tree structure**, where you can create folders and subfolders to group your entries by date, topic, mood, or any other criteria. You can also easily search, sort, filter, and rename your entries as you wish.

* Upload your diary entries to **OneDrive** in real-time, so that you can access them from any device and sync them across multiple platforms. 

* Choose from **multiple languages** to write your diary entries, such as English, 中文, 日本語, Español, Français, Deutsch. You can also switch between different languages easily within the app.

* Customize the appearance of your diary app with **multiple theme choices and dark mode**, where you can select from various colors and styles to suit your preference and mood. 

* Protect your diary entries with a **secure password**, so that only you can access them. You can also set a timeout period for the app to lock itself automatically after a certain amount of inactivity. You can also change or reset your password anytime within the app.

* Enjoy a **no-backend** design that ensures **absolute privacy** of your personal information. Light Diary does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data to any third-party servers or services. Your diary entries are stored locally on your device and encrypted on OneDrive.

Light Diary is the ultimate diary application for anyone who wants to keep a personal journal of their life. It is easy to use, feature-rich, and secure.
